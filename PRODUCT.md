# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Operations staff at asset-light freight forwarding SMEs in the Philippines. They use Neuron OS during active working hours — quoting, booking, tracking expenses, collecting payments. They are not technical; they expect clarity over cleverness. Admin users (Executive or HR) additionally manage team composition and user accounts.

## Product Purpose

Neuron OS is a full-lifecycle business tool: sales leads → quotations → contracts → operations bookings → accounting (billings, expenses, invoices, collections) → HR → executive reporting. It replaces spreadsheets and siloed tools. Success is measured by whether a small forwarding office can run their entire operation from a single interface without training.

## Brand Personality

Measured, trustworthy, purposeful. The interface should feel like a well-run operations desk — organized, fast to read, nothing decorative that doesn't earn its place.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with hero-metric cards and gradient numbers
- Glassmorphism or blurred panel effects
- Side-stripe accent borders on list items or cards
- Bright, consumer-startup color palettes

## Design Principles

1. **Hierarchy through structure, not decoration.** Labels, dividers, and whitespace create the reading order. Color and weight are reserved for status and action.
2. **Controls earn trust by being predictable.** Every button, toggle, and dropdown behaves the same across the app. No surprises.
3. **Errors are explicit and recoverable.** Every failure state names the problem and offers a path forward.
4. **Dense but not cramped.** Business tools need information density. Whitespace is intentional, not generous by default.
5. **Actions are irreversible until confirmed.** Anything destructive or hard-to-reverse requires a deliberate second step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Focus indicators must be visible. All interactive elements need keyboard access. Filipino business context — English UI is primary.
